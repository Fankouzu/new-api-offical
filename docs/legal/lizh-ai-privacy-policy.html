Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lizh AI ("Lizh AI", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our website, account services, API gateway, model marketplace, billing features, and support channels.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: username, display name, email address, password authentication metadata, account status, role, language, and settings.
  • Third-party login information: identifiers, usernames, display names, and email addresses from services such as GitHub or Discord when you sign in or bind an account.
  • API and usage records: API key identifiers, model names, request metadata, token counts, quota consumption, timestamps, status codes, latency, billing records, and abuse-prevention logs.
  • Payment and order records: top-up records, voucher redemption records, subscription records, payment provider references, invoice or order identifiers, currency, amount, and settlement status.
  • Device and technical data: IP address, browser type, user agent, referring pages, cookies, session identifiers, security events, and approximate location derived from network data.
  • Support communications: messages, attachments, contact information, and troubleshooting details you provide when contacting us.

2. API Content

API requests may contain prompts, inputs, files, images, outputs, or other content submitted by you or your applications. We process this content to provide the service, route requests to selected models or providers, perform billing, troubleshoot errors, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations. You should not submit sensitive personal data, secrets, credentials, regulated health data, payment card data, or other high-risk information unless you have confirmed that your use case and provider configuration are appropriate.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • create, authenticate, secure, and manage user accounts;
  • provide API access, route requests, calculate usage, and settle billing;
  • process payments, credits, vouchers, refunds, fraud checks, and disputes;
  • monitor uptime, performance, model availability, errors, and abuse risk;
  • provide customer support and communicate service notices;
  • improve product experience, pricing pages, onboarding, and documentation;
  • comply with legal, tax, accounting, sanctions, security, and enforcement obligations.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies, local storage, session cookies, and similar technologies to keep you signed in, protect against cross-site request forgery, remember preferences, attribute referrals, and measure service performance. We may use Google Analytics or similar analytics tools to understand public page visits, sign-up flows, API activation, and purchase conversion. Analytics events should not include raw API keys, raw voucher codes, passwords, or API secrets.

5. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • AI model providers and infrastructure providers that process API requests and host service components;
  • payment processors and commerce platforms that handle payments, vouchers, invoices, fraud checks, taxes, or disputes;
  • authentication providers such as GitHub or Discord when you choose to sign in or bind accounts;
  • analytics, security, logging, and monitoring vendors used to operate and improve the service;
  • legal or regulatory authorities when required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, and service integrity;
  • successors or affiliates in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or sale of assets.

6. International Processing

Lizh AI serves an overseas market, and your information may be processed in countries or regions different from where you live. Data protection laws may differ across jurisdictions. Where required, we use reasonable safeguards for international processing and vendor access.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the service, maintain billing and accounting records, resolve disputes, detect abuse, enforce agreements, comply with legal obligations, and operate security systems. API logs, billing records, and security records may be retained longer than ordinary account preferences because they are necessary for metering, reconciliation, fraud prevention, and auditability.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including access controls, hashed or tokenized identifiers where appropriate, transport security, operational logging, and credential protection practices. No system is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting your password, OAuth accounts, API keys, vouchers, and devices.

9. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, export, object to, or restrict certain processing of your personal information. You may also manage account settings, rotate API keys, unbind supported OAuth providers, or stop using the service. Some records may be retained where required for billing, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or legitimate security purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

Lizh AI is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to consent to online services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
